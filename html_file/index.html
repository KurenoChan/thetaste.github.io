✯——— Our Story ———✯

“A Taste, A Story”

The Taste, an American Hollywood-themed cinema restaurant, began on April 26th, 1996, founded by Chef Stanley Liew Seok Chang. Back then, the story begins with a small street food stall, selling fried chicken and burgers in front of a school on those days. Stanley’s father, Liew Cheng Huat, started this business with his family, together sustaining their livelihood. Chef Stanley was a student when he was 20 years old who studied abroad and met a group of friends who shared his aspirations. While they went on their separate ways, Emilia Rayne stayed with him and supported his dream. Stanley’s dream is to create a place where everyone could feel comfortable. To meet the need of the public, he dropped out of school and travelled to different countries to learn about their cultures. After a few years, Stanley learned culinary skills and decided to open a restaurant called “THE TASTE”, which was inspired by the famous American food show. He was obsessed with exquisite food on the show and couldn’t wait for others to taste it. However, Stanley struggled with funding, until Emilia and his other business partners joined to help him. Even his family, relatives, and neighbours supported him. He became more determined to pursue his dream and started his legend in a new city nearby.

Our Strength

Hygienic Food

Keeping our food served hygienic from time to time is our concern. We keep our operations on preparing and storing food or drink clean and under healthy conditions. We implement efficient cleaning procedures twice a day and our food is always served at a minimum temperature of 63°C.

Skillful Chefs

Serving good food to customers is our top priority. Our chefs are passionate about creating new recipes, planning menus, as well as ensuring the meals they serve are of good quality. Prioritizing food hygiene and implementing sanitary practices from time to time is our responsibility.

Movies & Environment

We opt for plush seating, moody tones, and dim lighting, played with relaxing Lo-fi music to create a cosy and comfortable dine-in ambience perfect for experiencing the cinematic restaurant environment. Our restaurants are ornamented with the 1990s American singers and actors’ wallpapers with a dartboard machine.

Events

We would love to bring more precious memories to our dear customers. Our yearly event, The Taste Fiesta, offers a great deal of up to 50% for our members to enjoy a better dining experience in our special rooms, and stand a chance to win a mysterious gift.
