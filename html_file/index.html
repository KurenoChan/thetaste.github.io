 Story Behind The Burgers 

In the heart of downtown Johor Bahru, Lazy Monday restaurant was born from the dreams of three burger enthusiasts with a passion for global culinary exploration. Inspired by their adventures in Austin, United States, and Tokyo, they set out to create the ultimate burger experience in their hometown. Since opening its doors in 2018, Lazy Monday has been committed to using locally sourced, fresh ingredients to craft mouthwatering burgers. From juicy grass-fed beef patties to artisanal brioche buns, every element is carefully chosen for maximum flavor. With a relaxed atmosphere and friendly service, Lazy Monday invites guests to indulge in the simple pleasure of a perfectly made burger. Whether opting for their signature classic burger or a gourmet creation with unique toppings, patrons are promised a culinary journey that satisfies and leaves them yearning for more.
