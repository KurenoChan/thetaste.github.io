Introduction

We are deeply grateful for your continued patronage. We cannot thank you enough for your unwavering support and trust with our team.

We’ve been known for crafting gourmet burgers and sides, starting with home delivery and expanding to retail outlets. Join us in savoring the leisurely spirit of Lazy Monday, where each bite embodies the simple pleasures of life.

Our Story

2018 - 2019

In 2018, Lazy Monday Kitchen (LMK) was born in downtown Johor Bahru, founded by Datuk Seri Syamsul Omar and his team of burger enthusiasts. Drawing inspiration from their global culinary adventures, they set out to redefine the burger experience in their hometown. LMK quickly gained popularity for its mouthwatering burgers made with locally sourced, fresh ingredients, offering a cozy atmosphere for guests to enjoy.

2020 - 2021

By 2020, Lazy Monday had evolved into Lazy Monday restaurant, expanding its menu and offerings under Datuk Seri Syamsul's leadership. However, the onset of the COVID-19 pandemic in 2021 brought unprecedented challenges. In response, Lazy Monday organized charity events, providing free meals to those in need as a testament to their commitment to community service during difficult times.

2022 - 2023

In 2022, Lazy Monday continued to thrive, opening its second branch in Iskandar Puteri, Johor, further solidifying its presence in the region. The expansion was a testament to Lazy Monday's growing popularity and success under Datuk Seri Syamsul Omar's guidance. In January 2023, Lazy Monday Tebing Dato Onn JB opened its doors, with Datuk Seri Syamsul Omar personally overseeing the grand opening ceremony, attended by Johor Chief Minister Datuk Onn Hafiz Ghazi.

2024

As Lazy Monday entered 2024, it stood as a symbol of culinary excellence and community spirit in Johor Bahru. With a commitment to quality and innovation, Lazy Monday continued to delight patrons with its gourmet burgers and friendly atmosphere under Datuk Seri Syamsul Omar's leadership. As Lazy Monday looked towards the future, it remained dedicated to providing unforgettable dining experiences and giving back to the community that had embraced it from the beginning.
